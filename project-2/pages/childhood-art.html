Childhood Art

I wanted to share artwork that I made in my childhood years. I think that it is important to see the progress and growth, even from when you were a child. I have found looking back at my artwork, that I always wanted to copy my grandma's style of art: abstraction. This meant that a lot of my art had lines, dots, and shapes that are unrecognizable. I also loved to recreate things that I knew well from my head, such as food, quotes, patterns, etc. I think it is fun to look back and see what I was creating at a young age compared to present day. It is cool to see what things have changed, and what has stayed the same.
