My Art

Custom Clothing

I fell in love with creating custom clothing. I was inspired by streetwear from stores online, and wanting to recreate clothing for myself by hand. I have created more custom clothing for other people than for myself. I have made sweatpants, sweatshirts, t-shirts, jeans, and more. In order to make a custom piece, I mix acrylic paint with a textile medium. The textile medium makes the paint secure to the fabric so that you are able to wash and dry the piece with no problem. I have received many requests to do custom clothing with anime characters on it. So many of my custom pieces I have made are more cartoon based.
