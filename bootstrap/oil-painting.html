My Art

Oil Painting

Oil paint is my favorite medium. I have only been working with it for 5 years. I have learned the origins of where the paint comes from, as well as techniques that work best. It is my favorite because it allows you to be messy in terms of blending, shading, and application. I typically use oil paint for more realistic paintings; this includes subjects such as portraits, insects, or still-lifes. Although, oil paint takes about 3 days to become completely dry. The first painting is one that I made for a friend. I started by creating a digital collage of 17 of his favorite album covers. I then transfered that to a canvas and used oil paint to recreate the collage.
