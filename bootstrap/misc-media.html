My Art

Misc. Media

These are images of artworks varying in different mediums. Some mediums include colored pencil, charcoal, watercolor, dark room photography, and digital art. I enjoy every medium for different reasons. For example, I like painting with watercolor because it is a slow process of building layers upon layer of paint, so you can achieve many different colors and shades. I enjoy digital drawing because it allows you to undo and redo easily. This helps with testing and experimenting with brush strokes, colors, shading, etc.
