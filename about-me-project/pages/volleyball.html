Start Of My Volleyball Journey

My volleyball career started in middle school. I played st Lundahl Middle school, and did summer camps. Then I transitioned to playing at Crystal Lake Highschool. While in my school off-season I played club volleyball at Sky High. I played many positions depending on the team: outside hitter, setter, and libero/defensive specialist. Although, because of my height, it was common that I was put as the libero. The libero is known for defense and passing. They are the player that wears a different colored jersey. This allows the libero to sub out with a hitter without having to be accepted by the referee.

Current Volleyball Participation

I do regret not playing volleyball in college, however I still stay active in the sport. I currently partcipate in 4 different leagues, and open gyms. I play beach volleyball through Kingston Lanes in the summer, as well as the fall. I play in a women's competitive league that is held through a middle school, and a men's competitive league that is held at Lifezone 360.

Why Volleyball Is My Favorite Sport

Volleyball is my favorite sport for many reasons. The first being that it forces you to be fully aware in the present. I also enjoy the cardio aspect of volleyball. My favorite part of the sport is the physical demand. I love throwing my body on the floor with volleyball rolls, hitting the ball front-row, and blocking at the net.
