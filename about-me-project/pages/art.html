What Sparked My Art Journey?

I dedicate the spark for my art journey to my Grandma. Since I was a little girl, I found interest in molding play-doh, creating dreamcatchers, and doing paint-by-numbers. This soon evolved into creating large canvas paintings, commissions, and custom clothing. Although I would not be the artist I am today without the molding of my Grandma's advice. I grew up with her by my side telling me that, it's okay to draw outside of the lines, it's okay to make a mess on the table, and it's okay to follow your creative intuition I even had my own designated craft drawer in her house that we bonded through. Her artistic style followed abstraction; unrecognizable figures and dots were her favorite.
